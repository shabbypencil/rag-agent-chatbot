Mandai Wildlife Reserve – Detailed FAQs (Placeholder)

Ticketing & Membership

Q1: Can I buy tickets online?
A1: Yes, tickets can be purchased online in advance. E-tickets are sent by email and can be scanned at park entrances.

Q2: Are there annual passes or memberships?
A2: A membership program offers unlimited visits to selected parks plus dining and retail discounts for a fixed yearly fee.

Q3: Are student or senior discounts available?
A3: Selected ticket types offer reduced prices for students, seniors, and children, subject to valid ID verification.

Transportation & Parking

Q4: Is parking available?
A4: Multi-storey and open-air car parks are available near each cluster of attractions. Parking charges apply by the hour.

Q5: Are shuttle buses provided?
A5: Shuttle buses connect nearby MRT stations to the reserve, with increased frequency during peak visiting hours.

Guest Services

Q6: Where can I find information once inside?
A6: Information counters are located near main entrances and central plazas across the reserve.

Q7: Are lockers available?
A7: Self-service lockers are available at selected locations for storing bags and personal items for a small fee.

Q8: Can I rent wheelchairs or strollers?
A8: Wheelchairs and strollers can usually be rented near entrances, subject to availability and a rental fee.

Park Etiquette & Safety

Q9: Can I feed the animals?
A9: Guests must not feed animals unless they are participating in supervised feeding sessions led by staff.

Q10: Are pets allowed?
A10: Pets are not allowed inside the wildlife parks for animal safety and hygiene reasons.

Q11: Are drones allowed?
A11: Recreational drone flying is prohibited within the reserve due to wildlife disturbance and safety concerns.

Booking & Presentations

Q12: How do I book show seats?
A12: For shows requiring reservations, guests use an online booking form on the same day, subject to limited seats.

Q13: What happens if I miss my booked show time?
A13: Late arrivals may be denied entry once the show begins, and seats are not guaranteed to be held beyond the start time.

Miscellaneous

Q14: Is Wi‑Fi available?
A14: Complimentary Wi‑Fi is available at selected zones near entrances, dining areas, and main plazas.

Q15: Can I host corporate events?
A15: Certain venues inside the reserve can be booked for corporate functions, with event packages handled by the sales team.
